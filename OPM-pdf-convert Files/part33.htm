Compliance Officer

Chris brings over twenty years of diverse Wall Street experience, both in bulge bracket and boutique settings, beginning his career at Merrill Lynch in Research and ECM/Investment Banking. He transitioned into trading and compliance at Sound Securities LLC and opened an international desk trading ordinary shares, and in 2010, he co-founded a proprietary trading firm utilizing various algorithms. Chris joined MacKenzie Partners Inc. in 2012 and co-founded the Sentinel Market Intelligence Group, primarily responsible for stock surveillance as well as proxy voting ahead of annual/special meetings of shareholders, including proxy contests and tender offerings. Chris graduated with a BS in Finance from Quinnipiac University.
